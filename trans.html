Transport

The school has a fleet of buses and it plies into the remote areas and towns. The bus has a complete coverage of places like Nagercoil, Thuckalay, Thickanamcode , Asaripallam, Rajakkamangalam, Mondaymarket, Colachal, Thiruvithancode and nearby areas. Our transport system strictly adheres to the norms laid by the State Government and ensures the safety of the students.






Hostel

Hostel is provided on request (Not Compulsory)

Fully secured hostel for girls with all facilities is functioning. Good, Palatable, Salubrious and hygienic food is offered to the students. Strict decipline and study hours are strictly maintained in the hostel. It is the responsibility of the parents to take up the children during the arrival and departure.
