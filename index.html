Welcome to
RV HR SEC School

RV School, a Co-Educational Higher Secondary School, Promoted by Arunai Educational and Charitable Trust is first of its kind in Dharmapuri District. RV School is recognized by the Directorate of School Education (Govt. of Tamilnadu).Recognition No.0000-0000-0000.

RV School is one of the top school in Dharmapuri district and Harur area. This school is know for its academic achievements, sports and extra curricular activities.

    Salient Features


  • School maintains strict discipline.
  • Student’s adaptation system & Bridge course for slow learners.
  • French Classes for +1 and +2 students.
  • Inbuilt Tuition Center for higher classes.
  • Separa text-startte Counselling Center for Boys and Girls.
  • Various clubs for encouraging Extra-Curricular activities.
  • Encouragement and constant support for extracurricular activities like Yoga, Silambam, Taekwondo, Classical & Western Dance, Music, Keyboard, Chess, Table tennis/Squash, Swimming, Gymnastic, Art & Craft etc.
  • Interested students are engaged in The Bharath Scouts & Guides and JRC.






Infrastructure


  • Amiable and amicable campus
  • Well equipped central computing facility.
  • Well established Physics, Chemistry, Computer, Botany & Zoology Laboratory.
  • English Language Lab for the mastery to gain communication skills with effective staff from IIAL (Staff from North India).
  • Smart classroom in all classes.
  • School Library has a sufficient number of books & serves as the power house for talents.

  • Partially residential for +1 & +2 girls.
